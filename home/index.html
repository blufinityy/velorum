About

Who We Partner With

Velorum works with ambitious Roblox developers, both emerging and established, who demonstrate strong fundamentals, scalable concepts, and long-term commitment. We back talent by prioritizing potential, execution, and sustainable growth over short-term returns.

  • Developers with strong gameplay fundamentals
  • Games with scalable growth potential
  • Teams committed to long-term development
  • Creators lacking capital, not talent

Process

Our Evaluation Process

We evaluate both execution quality and long-term potential. While live metrics are valuable, unreleased projects are assessed based on gameplay quality, design strength, and scalability vision.

01
Project Review

We assess gameplay fundamentals, development quality, originality, and overall execution.

02
Metrics Analysis

For live experiences, we evaluate retention, engagement, monetization signals, and scalability.

03
Growth Potential

For unreleased or early-stage projects, we evaluate audience alignment, expansion pathways, and revenue design.

04
Partnership Structuring

If accepted, we formalize a revenue-sharing agreement and initiate capital deployment alongside structured growth support.

Mission

Our Mission

I began developing on Roblox at the age of 11, drawn to the platform's creative freedom. Despite years of development on many projects, I lacked the capital and strategic knowledge required to scale my experiences to a real audience.

It wasn't until years later, through the connections I made along the way, that I began seeing sustained success. That gap between talent and leverage ultimately led to the creation of Velorum.

Velorum exists to identify and support exceptional developers who have the vision and executional ability, but lack financial backing. We believe talent should not be constrained by access to capital, and that growth should be built through aligned, performance-driven partnerships.

To reinforce that commitment, I am allocating over $50,000 from my own success to support and invest in emerging developers earlier in their journey, before their opportunity is lost and momentum fades.

- Ryan, Founder
